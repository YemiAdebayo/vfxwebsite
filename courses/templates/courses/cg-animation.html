Learn CG Animation At VFX Academy

The CG Animation Course at VFX Academy begins with a Foundation Module which gives students a thorough understanding of the basics of film making, visual effects and animation. This is then followed by an overall introduction to CG Animation.

At the successful completion of the Foundation Module & Overall Introduction to CG Animation, students can proceed to the Specialization Module. The full path to certification in CG Animation is outlined below.

Module 1: Foundation

3 Months Duration
Course Outline
  • Introduction to Matte Painting and the Role of Matte Painting in Visual Effects.
  • Introduction to Photoshop and Photoshop tools.
  • Introduction to After Effects.
  • Animation in After Effects.
Overall Introduction to CG Animation

This is the stage that introduces students to unique techniques in CG Animation and prepares them for Specialization in CG Animation. See all you are going to learn below:

Course Outline
  • Understanding 3D Space and Elements.
  • Introduction to Blender.
  • Camera Animation.
  • Introduction to Blender & Blender Tools.
  • Asset Layout in Blender.
  • Modeling Tools.
  • Basic UV Editing and Shading.
  • Using Photoshop for Texturing.
  • Texture Baking in Blender.
  • Creating and Exporting Game-ready Assets in Blender.

Module 2: Specialization

9 Months Duration

At the Specialisation level, the course strengthens the student’s technical skills acquired in Foundation and develop creative skills. It provides further depth into the technique and craft of cinematography, editing and sound. In this module, students shoot and edit anon-narrative film, which can be a music video or a documentary or an ad.

Modules 3 & 4: Pro Module Options

After successfully completing Foundation and Specialisation, students can choose from the following combination of Pro modules:

  • Pro Writing & Directing: Writing & Direction(I) + Writing & Direction (II).
  • Pro Cinematography:Production-Post Production + Cinematography Pro.
  • Pro Editing: Production-Post Production + Editing Pro.
